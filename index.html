About Stylish Cabinetry Millworks

Locally Rooted.
Globally Inspired.

As a leading player in the cabinetry business, Stylish Cabinetry Millworks accepts all sizes of cabinetry projects across Greater Vancouver and the Okanagan. We are a locally and family-owned studio dedicated to transforming spaces into works of art.

The best materials, the newest hardware, and the most updated technical and engineering knowledge are just our basic tools. Our brilliant customer service and the functionality of our design consistently lead us to outstanding outcomes and luxurious homes.

Founder & Master Craftsman
Behzad
Our Founder
"Every space we create carries our signature — not just in craftsmanship, but in the life and joy it brings to those who inhabit it."

With over a decade of hands-on experience in premium cabinetry and millwork across Europe and Canada, Behzad founded Stylish Cabinetry Millworks with a singular vision: to bring world-class craftsmanship to the homes of Greater Vancouver and the Okanagan.

What began as a passion for transforming spaces has grown into a team of dedicated artisans and designers who share that same commitment to excellence. We are proud to remain a family-owned business where every client receives the personal attention they deserve.
